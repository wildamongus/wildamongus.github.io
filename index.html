How to join?

1. Download Curseforge.

2. Create a new profile, make the Minecraft version 1.18.2 and the forge version 40.2.18.

3. Click create.

4. Wait for it to be done creating.

5. Click the 3 dots next to the play button.

6. Go in the mods folder and place the .jar mods there.

7. Close and reopen curseforge.

8. Click play!
